Solar Eclipse 14 dec. 2020

Chile is fortunate to have another total solar eclipse passing over it on monday 14 december 2020.
If you missed last year's then now is your chance, its totality will occur high in the sky across Chile's beautiful Lake District and the highly active Villarrica Volcano but partials of the eclipse will be visible throughout the country.
Visibility percentages by region: Visible parts of the eclipse by Chilean region, North to South:
  • Arica & Parinacota: 26%.
  • Tarapacá: 31%
  • Antofagasta: 42%
  • Atacama: 55%
  • Coquimbo: 66%
  • Valparaíso: 78%
  • Metropolitana: 78%
  • O'Higgins: 81%
  • Maule: 87%
  • Ñuble: 92%
  • Biobío: 94%
  • La Araucanía: 100%
  • Los Ríos: 100%
  • Los Lagos: 93%
  • Aysén: 79%
  • Magallanes: 54%
Trajectory of the eclipse: Larger Image and much more detailed information available on EclipseOphile

Tip! RV's/Campers are ideal to position yourself in the best spots for the eclipse while still "keeping a little distance"
